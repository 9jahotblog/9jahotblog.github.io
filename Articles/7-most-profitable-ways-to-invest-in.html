7 Ways to Invest in a yearJan 3, 2023

Though there are several Profitable investment options around us, but we must as well look for the most profitable offers. You must take you time in doing this to avoid being scammed by nefarious get-rich-quick schemes.

This article is curated to give you seven profitable ways to invest in 2023.

  • Plastic Recycling
  • Mutual fund
  • Real Estate
  • Oil and Gas
  • Fixed Deposit
  • Mining
  • Treasury bills

Plastic Recycling: Plastic recycling involves processing plastic waste into new and usable products. As Plastic disposal is a significant problem in the country, recycling plastic is a sure investment as you would provide the solution to plastic disposal and yield interest. You may not have the resources to help convert these plastics into new ones, but you could collect them and sell them to industries that deal with recycling plastics. You could be an employer of labor when you recruit others to help collect these plastics.

Mutual fund: Mutual funds are funds pooled from individuals by fund managers to invest in bonds.

Real Estate: You must have heard of Real Estate before now. Real Estate encompasses land and landed properties such as houses and other structures on the ground. Investing in Real Estate can occur in house flipping, property rental, and investment groups, among others. Real estate companies have made payment for properties easy by allowing a percentage of the price upfront with an agreement to amount the balance.

Oil and Gas: Nigeria and as a country is one of the largest producers of crude oil and presents a good prospect for investment. This oil and gas investment requires proper research, expert knowledge, and much paperwork. Nonetheless, investing in Oil and Gas ETFs and stocks is very profitable.

Fixed Deposit: Fixed deposit, as it is known, is the act of locking your money for the duration of its stake. Your money could be invested in banks for 30 days and above.

Mining: Minerals like Gold, Coal, limestone, bitumen, and Iron ore, are present in Nigeria. The mining sector is expanding rapidly as more investors enter this wealth pool.

Treasury bills: Treasury bills are low-risk investment schemes where government needs to borrow money for a maximum period of 364 days. Treasury bills are sold at a low face value and are provided by CBN. Generally, it is wise to consider these truths before investing.

Risk Tolerance: All investment comes with a level of risk. It will help if you understand your tolerance level before opting for any.

Goals and Target: Financial goals are either long-term or short-term. Long-term goals would be great if you intend to invest for retirement.

Stable source of income: When intending to invest, a stable source of income is vital, so you only invest some of what you currently have.

Research: It's important to know what you are signing up for as you invest so you don't bite your nails in regret. Finally, it's essential to do your due diligence when investing. Don't invest what you can't bear to lose.
