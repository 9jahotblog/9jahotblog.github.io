In this article, I would be talking about hypertension and what everyone should know about it. Hypertension, also known as high blood pressure is one of the major causes of untimely death in the world. It is a condition in which the pressure of the blood against the blood vessels is persistently high. It is a severe condition which can lead to death if not properly maintained.

Blood pressure is the force the blood exerts on the blood vessels through which it flows. Blood pressure is measured with a sphygmomanometer and it is read in two forms namely: systolic and diastolic numbers. The systolic number measures the pressure in the blood vessels when the heart contracts while beating. The diastolic number on the other hand measures the pressure in the blood vessels when the heart relaxes between beats.
