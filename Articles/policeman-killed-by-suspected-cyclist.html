Anger as policeman is killed by a suspected cyclist

The Oyo State Police Command has vowed to arrest some suspected cyclists who attacked and killed a traffic warden at Ibadan and then watched as motorist crush him.

The deceased, Mr Oluniyi Bamidele, who on Thursday held a commercial vehicle operator, for breaking the traffic law was killed by an oncoming top speed commercial vehicle who deliberately drove over him after being pushed by a commercial vehicle operator.

The command, who has immediately commenced an investigation into the incident, says it won't leave any stone unturned in apprehending those who deliberately caused the death of the traffic warden.

Tactical and other crime prevention teams have since been deployed to the area to forestall any untoward situation that can lead to the breach of peace in the state. The Command therefore enjoined the citinzery to go about their lawful business without any fear of Harassment or molestation.

Members of the public were encouraged to provide the police force with credible information and work with the Police force to bring these offenders to Justice..
