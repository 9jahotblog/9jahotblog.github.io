19th, Jan


Do you ever feel anguish? Does it feel physical? It isn't. It twists your guts, puts your mental defense in an emotional spiral. The pain prickles your skin with invisible pins, suppressing all the happy feelings beneath and there it sits at the throne of your mind. It screams more and more consuming your body, soul and mind. It makes you bow your head in the crowd, "You're the one who caused it", it would whisper. Reality slips, time slows down and everything becomes hard to grasp. Everyone says we would get over it, but do we really? We bury it at the deepest and darkest part of our minds wearing a mask till the pain becomes dull. This is for you, not for me.

Simi Precious


Have you seen this?

"Does he know?

Have you told him? That you're both from two different opposites world".

My subconscious asked as I was chatting with him. My love, the one who makes me smile and happy, the one I plan on spending the rest of my life with.

"Does he know you both have nothing in common? Does he know that while him and his siblings quarrel over shares of their father's property, you and your siblings quarrel over who gets to sleep on the ground or mat? Is it clear to him that while he eats just two slices of bread cause he's full, you eat two slices of bread because that's all you have? Does he know that while he doesn't really go out cause he's used to throwing his own parties, you don't go out because you have no presentable clothes and shoes?

Is it clear to him that while he's a rich prince, you're a poor pauper?.

I'm not saying you won't work out I'm just saying, let it be known to him that while he owns a whole damn city, you're just a random girl from a little village.
