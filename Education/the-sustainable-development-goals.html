THE 8 MILLENNIUM DEVELOPMENT GOALS

The 8 Millennium Development Goals are international development goals which aims at improving health, combat diseases, & eradication of poverty. It is a general declaration on Social Development launched in 2000 by the United Nations to shape the 21st Century & generations to come. Countries like China & Brazil met the goals & achieved a lot. China's poverty population deteriorated from 452 million to 278 million. India are also following these steps. Nepal is no longer the poorest country in South Asia due to this, Bangladesh isn't also limited to to this great improvements. Nigeria remains the most populous black nation on earth.

-Eradicate extreme poverty & hunger.

-Achieve universal primary education.

-Promote Gender Equality and empower women.

-Reduce Child Mortality

-Improve Maternal health

-Combat HIV/AIDS, malaria, and other diseases.

-Ensure environmental sustainability.

-Develop a global partnership for development.

How can we use broadcasting to solve the problems of Nigeria??

Establishing a Radio Station:

A radio programme which discusses about the trials, tribulations & darkest hours of Nigeria will be a good idea for solving the problems of the country. Journalists from different angles of the State will be invited & they will share their mindset about what has been going on, how it should be solved & how things should be done. If a website is created there must be a comment section where users tend to drop their opinions too so this means the programme should give space for the target audiences to drop their opinions & relate more on any issues. The government will definitely hear about the program, they will improve more once they hear about the facts stated by people in the programme.

Establishing a Television Programme:

A TV station is another good factor to solving the problems of the country. It will be a visual manner of using the Radio station, this will enable the programme launchers to express they're thoughts, feelings, ideas & visual representation of anything, items or videos. The audience can also watch the programme to acquire knowledge & also share their thoughts. If there's more reasonable stations like this, the country will prevail under bad circumstances.

ADVANTAGE OF THE PROGRAMMES

~ It will channel through mass communication.

~ It will have a intense impact on the emotions of the target audience.
