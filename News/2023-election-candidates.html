2023: LIST OF ALL OF THE 18 PRESIDENTIAL CANDIDATES..Jan 29, 2023

The long, awaited 2023 is here, and many are hopeful about what it brings. For many Nigerians, this year is significant because it is a decisive year for what becomes of the country in the next four years. Simply put, it is the year of the long awaited federal and state elections. Interestingly, this election is one of its kind because it necessarily must usher in a new person as the current President would be completing his second tenure this year. Also, the pre-election events and party primaries have seen the emergence of colossal figures who are undeniably have mammoth political followers. This reality resounds the question; Who will be the next president of the Federal Republic of Nigeria?

With the presidential elections scheduled for February 25, 2023, many Nigerians are ready to go cast their votes for the candidate of their choice.
