COACH ABUBAKAR HASSAN MAI ZUBARU SACKED BY EL KANEMI WARRIORS...Feb 25, 2023

Maiduguri Outfits, El kanemi Warriors on Thursday reported that they have sacked coach Abubakar Hassan Mai Zubaru and his technical crew after an unimpressive first half of the NPFL campaign.

The Desert Warriors managed only 5 points out of a possible 27, winning only one with two draws and six losses, scoring just 4 league goals and conceding 15, losing mammothly to Fidelis Ilechukwus' Plateau United on matchday 8 by five goals.

The two times FA cup champions returned to the NPFL for the first time since in 3 years after finishing top in the Northern conference. Former champions Bendel insurance and Bayelsa United also returned back to the Nigerian top tier league with NPFL debutants Doma United of Gombe.

The news of Abubakars' sack broke hours after their defeat at the El-kanemi stadium in a rescheduled fixture to Kwara United by 2 goals to 1. The club is expected to set up a new technical crew before the commencement of the second half of the NPFL season
