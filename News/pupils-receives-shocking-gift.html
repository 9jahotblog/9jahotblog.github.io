Pupils Recieve Shocking gift..

Amusan Abraham

Shocking gift...|| 13, June

Anambra school, ST. Paul Catholic church Egbeana Ezeawulu nibo awards pupils with goats for coming top In their classes. For being the best set of pupils in school, students were given unusual gifts which have never been received by students in the history of Nigerian education system.

It is important to note that the Nigerian education system is ranked 43rd in the world. While talking to a parent, she explained that it would have been better if they were not given a gift at all, as this is a deep stain to the education sector of the country.

While talking to a youth in the school community, he stated, "No doubt Nibo is an agrarain community, it is located behind Anambra state's governor's lodge with a reasonable population struggling to break even, but that does not mean that we cannot afford to give our kids items bigger than what we gave them. “See, we are in a season likened to be full of enjoyment and happiness, a season we use to recall the birth of Jesus Christ, the savior of the world. So we gave them a goat to either kill on Christmas day or sell it to buy anything, ”he said.
