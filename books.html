Letter To My Father

Author: Joy Daniel

About the Author:

Joy Daniel is an emerging voice in African literature, weaving personal and fictional narratives that resonate with readers across the continent and beyond.

Book Description:

Written in a single epistolary style, *Letter to My Father* is a blend of reality and imagination that captures the essence of a daughter's longing, reflection, and reconciliation. Through a series of heartfelt letters, Joy Daniel takes readers on an emotional journey of love, regret, and forgiveness.

Bridges

Author: Abraham Amusan

About the Author:

Abraham Amusan, also known as "uglywriter," is a seasoned poet whose works have captivated audiences with their raw emotion and depth. His poetry often explores themes of connection, separation, and the human experience.

Book Description:

*Bridges* is a collection of poetry that spans languages and cultures. It includes eight original poems and four features, making it a rich tapestry of thoughts and emotions. This book is a testament to Amusan's ability to bridge gaps—between languages, people, and ideas.

The Enchantress: Angela's Tale

Author: Kolade Philip O.

About the Author:

Kolade Philip O. is a versatile author who explores the realms of fantasy, romance, and Christian fiction. His work often delves into themes of transformation, power, and faith, captivating readers with both depth and creativity.

Book Description:

In *The Enchantress: Angela's Tale,* Angela's journey from grief to power unfolds in a story of love, loss, and magic. As a widow turned enchantress, Angela navigates a world where faith and magic collide, leading to a transformative adventure that challenges her very beliefs.
