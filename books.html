Letter To My Father

Author: Joy Daniel

Written in a single epistolary style, Letter to My Father is a blend of reality and imagination. Angela pens an emotive and emotional missive to her beloved father who, days to her adorable birthday, travels on a distant years-spanning journey. During his unpremeditated departure and absence, Angela, the main character recounts the mixture of ugly and beautiful experiences in a piece to her father. Her father's odyssey has really brought to the fore the true visages of friends, relatives and even foes. Will they overcome against all odds?

Best Seller

The Enchantress: Angela's Tale

Author: Kolade Philip O.

Angela is the central character who transforms from a grieving widow into a powerful enchantress. After her husband Anthony's death and the subsequent tragic loss of her daughters Taiwo and Kehinde, Angela is offered the chance to become the next enchantress. This role imbues her with the ability to control destinies and devour humans. But it also comes with a heavy burden and the temptation of vengeance. Did her Joy as an enchantress become short-lived too? Find out. There are lessons, betrayals, and gripping plot twists.
