Letter To My Father

Price: ₦1,000

Author: Joy Daniel

About the Author:

Joy Daniel is a budding Nigerian writer. She holds a certificate in English Education at the Premier University, the University of Ibadan.

Before this publication, she has been writing short stories, poems, and articles on her blog (JoyWrites) since 2020. Lucid Dream and Other Stories is her first published book.

Asides from writing, Joy loves to teach, travel and cook.

Book Description:

Written in a single epistolary style, Letter to My Father is a blend of reality and imagination that captures the essence of a daughter's longing, reflection, and reconciliation. Through a series of heartfelt letters, Joy Daniel takes readers on an emotional journey of love, regret, and forgiveness.

The Enchantress: Angela's Tale

Price: ₦1,500

Author: Kolade Philip O.

About the Author:

Kolade Philip O. is a versatile Author, storyteller, and creative professional. Born and raised in Lagos, Nigeria, and has since pursued a variety of creative endeavors, including web design, social media management, and tutoring. He currently teaches English and Literature in senior secondary schools, amongst other lessons across his vicinity.

Book Description:

In The Enchantress: Angela's Tale, Angela is the central character who transforms from a grieving widow into a powerful enchantress. After her husband Anthony's death and the subsequent tragic loss of her daughters Taiwo and Kehinde, Angela is offered the chance to become the next enchantress. This role imbues her with the ability to control destinies and devour humans. But it also comes with a heavy burden and the temptation of vengeance. Did her Joy as an enchantress become short-lived too? Find out. There are lessons, betrayals, and gripping plot twists.
