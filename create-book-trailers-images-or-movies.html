Choosing the right style.

  • 3D animation: Ideal for sci-fi, Fantasy, and adventure stories.
  • Anime: Perfect for manga-style adaptations and graphic novels.
  • Realism: Suitable for dramas, thrillers, and historical fiction.
  • Mixed media: Combine live-action with animation or CGI elements.
  • Watercolor: A dreamy, ethereal style that uses watercolor-inspired visuals to bring the story to life.
