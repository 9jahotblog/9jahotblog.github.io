Data Deletion Instructions

To delete your data, please follow these steps:

  1. Go to our website: 9jahotblog (logged in)
  2. Click on the "Account" button in the top right corner
  3. Scroll down to the "Data Deletion" section
  4. Click on the "Delete My Data" button

Alternatively, you can contact our support team at support to request data deletion.
