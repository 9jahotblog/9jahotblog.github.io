unit1: WHAT IS A WHATSAPP ROBOT?

A WhatsApp Robot is a chatbot that makes chatting fun & whatsapp very easy to use. Promotions, business, conversational marketing, sales.. e.t.c. Technically, WhatsApp robots come in different forms, many companies buy WhatsApp bot to help strengthen the bond of the customers to the organization. The organization may not be able to reach out to the customers in time, but WhatsApp bots come in handy in such terms. WhatsApp itself has an inbuilt special bot for special purposes created for Whatsapp business users so they can be able to manage their business with ease. It does not only contain a bot that replies instantly, but also a cart to make shopping more easier for customers. The bot am talking about here is (Asena) a repository on github that can be deployed to heroku & merged into your WhatsApp account, although Asena doesn't login to your account, but makes WhatsApp fun & easy to use. Let's see what it can do!
