WHAT IS AFFILIATE MARKETING?

It is a simple business model that involves promoting other people's products or services and getting a paid commission for doing so...

Example of Affilate Marketing

In a lame mans term:- One way or the other, you're marketing something but you don't get paid, you bought a roll of sausage, your friend tasted it, he asked where you got it, you referred him, he went there to buy for himself and friends and then, the referrals keep spreading but you weren't paid, you just advertised a product to your friend without the company knowing or paying you i.e Affilate Marketing.
