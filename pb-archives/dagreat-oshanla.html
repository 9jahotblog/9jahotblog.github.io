Meet Dagreat fast rising superst..Jan 29, 2023
  • Full Name: Adewale Muees Odekoya
  • Nickname:Dagreat Oshanla
  • Residence:Lagos, Nigeria
  • Birthday: 4th of July 1995

Da great is a fast rising artist with copious amounts of tracks recorded, & has been in the music industry for years. He spent his childhood in Bariga, Somolu, hustling, to make ends meet.

He lives by the quote "Slow mo better than do fast fast" which means slow & steady is much Profitable than unecessary hurry, which he finds a motivational influence, therefore giving him the courage to better respond to threats & opportunities that emerges in his industry while things change & while he grows.

The singer is also found to be an introvert, he endears his own company with some close friends & family, Preferably, he enjoys the indoors more. Oshanla is seen to be hardworking, ambitious, & very much determined on whatever comes his way in music, he works hard on every music he makes which enables him stand out, amongst other rising artist. Additionally, he has a similar voice with "Wande Coal".

One of the best personality trait embedded in dagreat is fun. The people he hangs out with loves this trait, & always share video captioning how much they enjoy his company with such approaches to everyone. He has featured top artists, producers, & Disk Jockey's like Pizzle, DJ Enimoney & Skales e.t.c.
