Bridges - The ER Bridges is the third extended readlist from the desk of Amusan Abraham - uglyWRITER. The book features Trish man as the first poem which is written in an Indian language, titled fondness, followed by "the way we were brought up", then "Wetin man go do " in pidgin, dark futures, good things just take time and Happy people were all written in English, bridges which was a bonus poem was featured with Helen.
