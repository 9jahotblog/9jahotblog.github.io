The Celestial Seal: Angels vs Demons

Fantasy Novel! Stream, Read, Download, & more..

About the Book...

The Celestial Seal: Angels vs Demons is a recently released fantasy novel that features Doscendeles, a powerful demon, sent by the Demon King to steal the Celestial Seal of the Sanctified Souls realm.

However, Doscendeles has a change of heart and decides to hide the seal, preventing the Demon King from misusing it. He also steals the Celestial Seal of the Demonic Realm, rendering his master vulnerable.

The Demon King, enraged by Doscendeles' betrayal, pushes him into a pit of boiling fire, but not before Doscendeles reveals that he has hidden the seals in the human world. The King, realizing his vulnerability, absorbs the dark energy of his dissent in his followers, to become more powerful and summons two dark demons (Vex and Torment) to retrieve the Celestial Seal at all costs.

Throughout the story, the Celestial Seal is revealed to be an ancient artifact that safeguards realms from destruction and chaos. Without it, a realm is vulnerable to attack. Doscendeles' actions have set in motion a chain of events that could lead to a new era of peace or UNLEASH UNPRECEDENTED CHAOS.
