Godot

Who is Godot Thompson??

Godot is a supernatural being, residing in another universe under the earth. Due to his fastidious & deviant character, he is released to a more complex world known as "The human world". Godot will have to live & die 7 times to learn the true meaning & basics of life. And within or before the 7th life, he must've internalized the lessons & wiseness in life, which will grant him the grace to answer just one question the godfather of his world has been asking him. If he is able to answer this question, he will be accepted with open arms together with a higher post in his world. He came into life as the richest man at first, second life a boxer, third life a singer, all was superb & Egoistic, but there's this one life, which was dreadful. Did this life make him change his arrogant nature? Find out.

Title: The Tale of Godot Thompson

Writer: Kolade Philip Ogunlana.

Accessibility: Everyone --Unpublished

Godot's 7th Life (Synopsis)

Focuses on a bizarre, hectic, & dreadful life of a man who was destined to be great but the friends he surrounds himself with are all fiends who seeks his downfall. Anticipate how an unexpected child paves his way down to success through the obstreperous & ferocious world.
