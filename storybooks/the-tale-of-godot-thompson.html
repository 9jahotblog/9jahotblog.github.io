The Tale of

GODOT THOMPSON

The Tale of Godot Thompson is one of Ogunlana Kolade Philip's stories that focuses on a bizarre, hectic, & dreadful life of a man who was destined to be great but the friends he surrounds himself with are all fiends who seeks his downfall.
